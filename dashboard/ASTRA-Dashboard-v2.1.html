Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture adapted from LeWorldModel (Maes, Le Lidec, Scieur, LeCun, Balestriero 2026). Encoder maps SNN state → 64-dim latent space. Predictor models dynamics conditioned on spike actions. SIGReg prevents collapse. CEM planner optimizes spike injection in latent space. Training: L = L_pred + λ·SIGReg(Z) — single hyperparameter λ.
